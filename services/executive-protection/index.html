Executive Protection Services

Discreet, professional protection for corporate executives, dignitaries, and high-profile individuals — combining proactive risk intelligence, meticulous planning, and highly trained protection specialists.

Request a quote Download capability brief →

We deliver discreet, professional protection services for corporate executives, dignitaries, and high-profile individuals requiring enhanced personal security. Our approach combines proactive risk intelligence, meticulous planning, and highly trained protection specialists to safeguard client safety and ensure uninterrupted operational continuity.

Each protection program is tailored to the client's profile, itinerary, and evolving threat environment, with strict emphasis on precision, discretion, and confidentiality.

Coverage

Key areas of coverage.

  • Principal threat and vulnerability assessment
  • Advance work and venue reconnaissance
  • Close protection teams and detail leadership
  • Secure transportation and driver programs
  • Residence and family protection programs
  • Travel planning and route security
  • Crisis response and medical preparedness
